TechStardle

Welcome to the TechStardle! You will be given a daily UKG trivia question, and it's your job to find the right answer!

Special Rules

You can use any resource you want to help you solve the question. This includes any ukg domain specific URL or source. We can't stop you from using other sources, but we can be disappointed!

Look for your name on the leaderboard to see your place in the UKG standings. Every 15th and 30th (28th/29th on February) we'll give the top 3 a UKG reward! See the reward section for more details on those.

Instructions: Simply choose the right answer to the question from the list of choices and hit Submit! Your concurrent score will automatically be calculate and tallied!
